About Us

Established in 2009 Our group meets weekly for dance lessons and social dancing on Tuesday nights (7:00-9:30PM) at Cal Arts Academy (on Blackstone Ave., between Shaw and Gettysburg Avenues). We have three simultaneous dance classes, ranging from beginners to intermediate, followed by an open dance. We also dance anywhere and everywhere there might be some excuse to swing dance! The Swing Shift at River Park Fresno Swing Dance Community brings you The Swing Shift every THIRD MONDAY for FREE dancing at River Park in front of Teazer World Tea Market during a part of "Get Moving Mondays". All dances/dancers are welcome, especially Lindy Hop, Charleston, Balboa and East Coast Swing! This is a family friendly event. We hope to see you out there! Fresno Swing Dance is a grassroots community that regularly teaches swing dancing and performs all throughout the Central Valley.

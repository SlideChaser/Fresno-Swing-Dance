Lindy Hop at Art Hop with Fresno Swing Dance! Free dancing at the new Cultural Arts Park in
downtown Fresno. Every FIRST THURSDAY of the month, from 7:00-9:00pm

What to expect:

  • Swing music
  • Swing dancing
  • Food trucks
  • Nearby art venues
  • Free parking

ArtHop takes place the first Thursday Evening (5-8PM) of every month in downtown Fresno.
There are over 35 listed art/music venues that participate. Several venues are within walking
distance of each other. To learn more, visit Fresno Arts Council

Fresno Swing Dance Community brings you The Swing Shift at River Park every THIRD MONDAY for free
dancing at River Park in front of Teazer World Tea Market during a part of "Get Moving Mondays".
All dances/dancers are welcome, especially Lindy Hop, Charleston, Balboa and East Coast
Swing! This is a family friendly event. We hope to see you out there!
